#When to use Couchdb
* Data modeled as complete documents
* Freedom of going "schema-less"
* Horizontal scaling

!SLIDE bullets incremental transition=scrollLeft
# When NOT to use Couchdb
* Very strict definitions
* On the fly queries
* Transactions
* Eventual Consistency
